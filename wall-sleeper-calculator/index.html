Assumptions/Rules

  • Sleepers are 2m long and 200mm high.
  • The Wall Length must be a multiple of 2, wall heights do not (and can have decimal places).
  • When dealing with slopes, if the slope height at the start or end of a wall segment exceeds 100mm, a sleeper is required.
  • This tool is accurate and mathematically sound.
  • The total area refers to the total area of sleepers, which may be slightly larger than the total area of the defined wall (due to sleeper requirements).
